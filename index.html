  • Start by typing in a card name. If you don't know any, try typing "Galea" in the non-land cards search bar. Click "Search"
  • An image will appear with information about the card, as well as its attack and toughness
  • Under the card, there are two boxes labeled "Attack" and "Toughness". These are adjustable so that the card can be adjusted for sorceries and equipments that affect these factors
  • Clicking a card will tap it, meaning it can't be used until the owner's next turn.
  • Double-clicking a card will destroy it, meaning the card has either been killed, sent to the graveyard, or exiled.
  • The land cards work in a similar way, except they are only used as payment to cast creatures, enchantments, equipments, and other non-land cards
  • For basic land cards, you can click the "Add __________" instead of typing in the card's name
